Why WebX?

WebX

WebX is a simple web browser built on Python. No extensions, no extra features, only browsing!


Development

Development was first started by Orlando Huang in 2020. Together with other volunteers from all around the world at Jiusoft, now it has a history and bookmark system; and reload, back, and forward functions. But don't think that we're stopping here. We are still planning to add more features, so stay tuned!

Why you will like WebX


Everything you need

WebX has everything you need. Bookmarks, history, basic snake game, and a homepage with a beautiful background.


See the code yourself

This project is open source and free software, licensed under GPL-3. All code for this project is on Github. This means you can modify and customize the code as needed.


Your data is in your hands

We do not have nor use your data in anyway. Your history and bookmarks are saved in a local file, situated on your computer. Your history and bookmarks are not saved on the internet. The only copy of your history and bookmarks is in your hands, except if a third-party collects that information (like your internet provider).
